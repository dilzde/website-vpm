Our Vision

VPM International was founded with a vision to spread Yahweh's Prophetic word and bring spiritual guidance to all who seek it. For over 15 years, we have been a beacon of hope, faith, and spiritual awakening in Kenya and beyond.

Our Mission

Our mission is to deliver accurate prophetic messages, provide spiritual guidance, and create a supportive community where members can grow in their faith and understanding of Yahweh's plan for their lives.
